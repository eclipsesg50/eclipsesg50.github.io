What can be improved in our project?

We think that we can focus more on the design and aesthetics of the launcher as ours is too plain.

What went wrong and what did we do to solve it?

At fisrt, our plane could not fly far. We thought it is the launcher's problem but we eventually realised it is the paper plane problem. Thus, we decided to use another design and this time it can fly rather far away.

What could have been done better?

Besides focusing on the sructurere of the launcher, we should focus mor on the paper airplane itself and test a few times before the competition so we can find the angle and design of the paper airplane with which can our distance be further
